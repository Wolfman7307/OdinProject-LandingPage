Did you know...

When brake pads are routinely inspected, they can be replaced before displaying any anomalies to guarantee your vehicle can execute the most efficient, safest stops as possible.

The brake pads are made from a friction material designed to reduce the kinetic energy necessary to safely slow and stop the vehicle.

Brake pads are typically designed with a low-wear indicator which will cause a squealing noise when the brakes are applied.

When the friction material on the brake pads wear out, the backing plate will come into contact with the rotor and cause an unsafe condition. The vehicle will no longer be able to safely stop as expected.
